Rani Lakshmibai



Rani Lakshmibai was one of the bravest freedom fighters of India . She was the Queen of Jhansi and played a key role in the Revolt of 1857. Known for her courage and leadership, she fought fearlessly against British rule and became a symbol of strength and patriotism.

Timeline of Rani Lakshmibai

1828

Born as Manikarnika (Manu) in Varanasi

1842

Married to the King of Jhansi and became Rani Lakshmibai.

1851

Gave birth to a son, but he sadly died in infancy.

1853

Her husband, the King of Jhansi, passed away.

1854

British applied Doctrine of Lapse and annexed Jhansi.

1857

Actively participated in the Revolt of 1857 against British rule.

1858

Died fighting bravely in battle, becoming a legend forever.
